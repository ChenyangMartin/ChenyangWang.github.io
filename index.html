About Me

I am a Lecturer in Econometrics with research interests in Applied economics. I am affiliated with the University of Manchester.

My work focuses on understanding liquidity constraints and entrepreneurship and role of investor sentiments played in Chinese stock market. I teach undergraduate and postgraduate modules in Introduction to Econometrics and Further Econometrics.
